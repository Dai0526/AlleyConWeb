What is

ALLEYCON

ALLEYCON is a one-day technology event where the world's most successful founders and investors share behind-the-scenes stories about their pivotal decisions, like:


  • Should they launch their venture or fund? Why? And How?
  • What are practical tactics to grow? When is it time to pivot?
  • How do they keep reinventing the venture? Should one sell?

Whether you're an investor or a consultant or techie, you're confronted with hard decisions everyday. Over the course of an unforgettable day, ALLEYCON brings you practical lessons on how to make those tough calls.
